Battle Pass

Apex Legends also has a battle pass system coming in its first season, which starts in March. Some things that are included in the battle pass system are:

  1. New Gun Skins
  2. More Loot Boxes On Leveling
  3. Crafting Metals You Can Earn
  4. Rewards For Winning, And Other Feats
  5. Challenges To Expand On The Gameplay
  6. Expansions On The Story line

Developers of Apex Legends:
Respawn Entertainment

Respawn Entertainment, LLC is an American video game development studio founded by Jason West and Vince Zampella. West and Zampella previously co-founded Infinity Ward and created the Call of Duty franchise, where they were responsible for its development until 2010.
